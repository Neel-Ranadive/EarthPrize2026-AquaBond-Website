Our Mission

Everyday, 40 million people suffer from chronic wounds, with regions like the Sub-Saharan having infection risks reaching 18% from the lack of hygienic care. Our mission is to break restricted access to proper aid and reduce the environmental impact of PFAS/forever chemicals from 65% of bandages worldwide.
