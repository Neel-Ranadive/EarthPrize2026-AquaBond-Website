Why AquaBond

Aquabond serves as a fusion in medicine and biotechnology, while using bio-inspired engineering to combat hazardous waste concerns worldwide. The prefix Aqua- represents the living aquatic foundation of our bandages, using the Spirulina and Chlorella Algae cells to oxygenate and nourish the wound site. The suffix -Bond aims to signify the bandage's revolutionary potential with its biomimicry-inspired adhesive mechanism, which replaces harmful, PFAS-laden chemical glues with a gecko-inspired structural imprint. By mimicking the microscopic textures found in nature to create physical adhesion, AquaBond not only ensures a toxin-free and secure hold, but both preserves human and environment health from its biodegradable qualities.
